Life of Ashley Hayden

My whole life I have been surrounded by art. I enjoy going to museums, taking pictures using my photography skills and edgtiting,and especially creating things with my hands! Since middle school I have dreamed of graduating from an art school! I was not sure what I would major in but I knew belonged with in a creative community. Currently I am a freshman attending the Cleveland Institute of Art. My first semester here was was an explosion of art. I was exposed to so many foundational classes to help explore which career path I wanted to go down for art.

I am very interested in becoming a Product/UX/UI designer. I fell in love with the concept of solving problems to make the lives of consumers more convenient. Furthermore, I am fascinated with how we interact and respond to design daily. Along this journey, I am learning that the things that you can do and create in design are infinite; all you need is a vision, and you can make it come to life!
